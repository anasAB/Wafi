A — صفحة كاملة (Route)

Settings is a full-screen page at /settings. Back button returns to wherever you came from. Clean, standard, works great on mobile. URL is bookmarkable/deep-linkable.

B — Bottom Sheet (Modal)

Settings slides up as an overlay. No navigation, no URL change. Feels native on mobile. But sub-screens (e.g. Shop Profile editor) need to push inside the sheet — gets complex with 17 stories' worth of content.

💡 The epic spec describes a gear icon in the global header that opens a full settings experience with 11 sub-screens. A full-screen route handles that depth much better than a sheet.
